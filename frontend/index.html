Geopolitical Relief Lifts Stocks to Eighth Consecutive Weekly Gain. The S&P 500 closed up 0.37% on Friday, completing its eighth straight week of gains as investors responded positively to signs of progress in US-Iran peace negotiations. US Secretary of State Marco Rubio said there were "good signs" that an agreement is within reach. Treasury yields moderated from recent peaks, providing relief to equity markets that had been pressured by higher borrowing costs.
